20 Phrases That Will
Save You in China

中文 · Pinyin · English · Cultural Tips
A free guide from China Travel with Ease
Greetings & Politeness
1
你好
Nǐ hǎo
Hello
Smile and nod = instantly warmer
2
谢谢
Xièxie
Thank you
Use anytime, anywhere — never wrong
3
对不起
Duìbuqǐ
Sorry / Excuse me
Also works when squeezing through crowds
4
你会说英语吗？
Nǐ huì shuō Yīngyǔ ma?
Do you speak English?
Works best at hotels, airports, info desks
Dining & Food
5
这个多少钱？
Zhège duōshǎo qián?
How much is this?
Point at the item while asking
6
不要辣
Bú yào là
No spicy / Not spicy
Lifesaver in Sichuan and Hunan provinces
7
买单
Mǎi dān
The bill, please
Raise your hand slightly — standard gesture
8
好吃！
Hǎo chī!
Delicious!
Chefs and waiters love this — genuine smile guaranteed
Getting Around
9
我要去这里
Wǒ yào qù zhèlǐ
I want to go here
Show map on phone + say this = perfect combo
10
出租车！
Chūzūchē!
Taxi!
Wave hand at roadside taxi stands
11
请打表
Qǐng dǎ biǎo
Please use the meter
Say right after getting in to avoid price disputes
12
地铁站在哪？
Dìtiě zhàn zài nǎ?
Where is the subway station?
Most subway signs have English — this phrase gets you there
Shopping & Money
13
太贵了
Tài guì le
Too expensive
The universal opening move for bargaining
14
便宜一点
Piányi yìdiǎn
Cheaper, please / A little cheaper
Follow up after "too expensive" — negotiations begin
15
可以微信/支付宝吗？
Kěyǐ Wēixìn/Zhīfùbǎo ma?
Can I pay with WeChat/Alipay?
China is nearly cashless — this is the real question
Emergency & Help
16
救命！
Jiù mìng!
Help! (life-threatening)
Only for real emergencies — gets immediate attention
17
医院在哪？
Yīyuàn zài nǎ?
Where is the hospital?
For medical needs — pharmacies are everywhere too
Hotel & Practical
18
WiFi密码是什么？
WiFi mìmǎ shì shénme?
What's the WiFi password?
Most Chinese hotels have free WiFi — just ask
19
洗手间在哪？
Xǐshǒujiān zài nǎ?
Where is the bathroom?
The word you'll use most after "hello" and "thank you"
20
再见
Zàijiàn
Goodbye
End every interaction with warmth
